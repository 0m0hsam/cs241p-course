Supplementary Material

In previous semesters, students read about inheritance from the textbook, "Python 3 Object-oriented Programming" by Dusty Phillips. This is available electronically from our library subscription here.

If you would like, you can reference this electronic textbook for more information about the topics. Here are the appropriate sections:

  • From Chapter 1, from the section "Composition and Inheritance" until the end of the chapter. In my copy this is pp. 17-32. This reading presents inheritance at a conceptual level.

  • From Chapter 3, from the beginning until (but not including) Multiple Inheritance. In my copy this is pp. 63-68. This reading demonstrates how to implement inheritance in Python.
